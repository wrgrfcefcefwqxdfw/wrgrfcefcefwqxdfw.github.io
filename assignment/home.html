Final Thoughts

Design: 10/10 - Even with my shoddy painting, this model still look absolutely stunning.

Articulation: 6/10 - It is not that great. However, it can still get into many dynamic action poses, so it is not that bad. It would benefit if all the joints had double points in them, but it is probably too costly to do so.

Play factor: 9/10 - The kit is as solid as a rock. It is not as flimsy as some other kits, and the waist joint is not so tight that it will break off like the MG version. You can play with it however you like and it will not break apart instantly. Its joints can hold up any pose you like and can definitely withstand the test of time.

Build: 8/10 - It is a shame that the gold parts are not separate pieces but are stickers. While the stickers are all well and good, painting it would definitely make it look much better. The rest of the building process is very simple and straight forward, and there are even clever engineering in some areas.

Accessories: 8/10 – Good variety of accessories are included, however could be improved with a couple more expressive hands or perhaps a bazooka.

Overall: 10/10 – Good looks, good details. I can't see why you would NOT buy this unless you are have the MG version. This kit is extremely affordable, at about ¥2,600 ! So if you haven’t got a Sinanju, get this. But even if you have the MG version, you could always buy another right? :p
